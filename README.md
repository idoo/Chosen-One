# Chosen-One

## About
Chosen-One is a playful iOS application that lets you experience life as a member of a secret organization. Each day, you receive mysterious missions—some absurd, some challenging—that keep you engaged, active, and entertained. Complete daily tasks, level up your fitness, and share your reactions with friends.

## Features
- Daily secret missions and challenges  
- Fitness tasks disguised as fun (push-ups, squats, etc.)  
- Progressive difficulty for all fitness levels  
- Simple, dark-themed, immersive interface  

## How It Works
Open Chosen-One to discover your daily secret mission. Each day brings new tasks—some mysterious, some physical. Gradually increase your fitness level while having fun with the game of secrets. Share your experiences with friends or capture reactions to complete the immersion.

## Privacy Policy
Chosen-One respects your privacy:  
- All mission and fitness data is stored locally on your device  
- No personal information is required  
- No data is collected or shared with third parties  

## Permissions
Chosen-One requires the following permissions:  
- Notifications: To send you daily secret missions and reminders

## FAQ

### Why am I not receiving notifications?
Please ensure:  
1. Notifications are allowed for Chosen-One in your device settings  
2. Your device is not in Do Not Disturb mode  
3. System-wide notifications are not disabled  

### How are fitness tasks tracked?
Fitness tasks are for personal tracking only. You manually mark them as completed within the app.
